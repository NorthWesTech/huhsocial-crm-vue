SERVICES
Activities

During the Campaign, Ambassador will:

  • participate in at least one (1) activity arranged by Lanistar, the nature of which will be notified in advance to the Ambassador by Lanistar, during which the Ambassador will create their Social Content (as defined below) to be used in real time on Ambassador's Channels (as defined below) and which for the avoidance of doubt shall be additional to the Social Content requirements detailed below. The activity will take place at a time and date notified to Ambassador, at a venue selected by Lanistar;
  • participate in the launch event organised by Lanistar at a time and date notified to Ambassador by Lanistar at least 4 weeks prior to the event date, and at a venue selected by Lanistar, during which the Ambassador will create their Social Content to be used in real time on Ambassador's Channels (as defined below) and which for the avoidance of doubt shall be additional to the Social Content requirements detailed below. Attendance at the launch event is compulsory ( Lanistar may grant exemptions to this at its sole discretion for valid reason).

For the avoidance of doubt the Social Content created pursuant to this section, Activities, shall be subject to the Disclosure Requirements and Timing Requirements set out below but shall not be subject to the Approval Requirements set out below.

Social Content

Ambassador shall utilise their standing on social media via accounts owned and controlled by Ambassador including (but not limited to) Instagram, Snapchat, Facebook, Twitter, TikTok, YouTube, LinkedIn and such other channel(s) as Lanistar may specify from time to time (each a " Channel") to deliver Campaign messaging. As a minimum Ambassador will post the following stories and grid/feed posts:

  • one scripted post on the Campaign Start Date (to be provided by Lanistar and posted in accordance with Lanistar’s instructions) on an agreed Channel; the Ambassador acknowledges that all Lanistar influencers will be posting an identical post on the Campaign Start Date and its accuracy (both content and timing) is of the essence; and thereafter
  • per month during the Campaign a minimum of 4 organic posts on the agreed Channel (content to be creatively and authentically produced by Ambassador to be posted in accordance with Lanistar’s instructions and subject to Lanistar’s prior approval); and
  • from time to time during the Campaign, scripted captions (to be provided by Lanistar) on an agreed Channel,(collectively " Social Content").
Social Content

Social Content

  • All posts will make clear the commercial relationship between Ambassador and Lanistar. In particular Ambassador shall use the #ad handle in each post in a clear and upfront manner and always at the beginning of the post.
  • The Ambassador shall not attempt to hide or in any way disguise the use of the #ad handle. Viewers should not be required to click on, scroll down or move their mouse over a link in order to view the #ad handle. In particular, where Social Content includes vlogs or videos, Ambassador shall use the #ad handle at the beginning of the title of the video/vlog and shall ensure that every occurrence of the video/vlog on a social network or otherwise (including in thumbnail form) displays the #ad handle.
  • The #ad disclosure is required regardless of any space limitations of the post (e.g. Twitter). Ambassador shall comply with any other disclosure requirements or guidance from Lanistar from time to time and shall ensure that all posts conform to current good practice and any relevant Advertising Standards Authority guidance and rulings.
  • Lanistar may request that Social Content is removed or edited at any time and Ambassador shall promptly comply with any such request.

Approval Requirements

Ambassador must submit all Social Content to Lanistar for approval prior to posting. Social Content relating to the Campaign launch shall be submitted at least 14 days in advance of the Campaign Start Date. All other Social Content shall be submitted a minimum of 7 days in advance of intended posting. Lanistar may make any changes to the Social Content as it deems necessary and where required Ambassador shall re-submit such modified Social Content to Lanistar for further approval. Once approved Ambassador shall post the Social Content in the agreed form.

Timing Requirements

All Social Content will be posted within the time periods discussed and agreed with Lanistar (based on data relating to Ambassador’s interactions).

Any such issue of C Shares shall be subject to the conditions set out in the Payment Timetable detailed below and Ambassador issuing Lanistar with an invoice for the nominal value of the shares, being the cash equivalent of the Referral Bonus. Again, such invoice (the " Bonus Invoice") should reflect the fact it is to be settled by the issuance to the Ambassador of the proposed number of C Shares.

For the avoidance of doubt no Referral Bonus shall be payable to the Ambassador or Agency in respect of any individual already known to or in contact with Lanistar in relation to the actual or potential provision of influencer marketing services. Lanistar shall have the sole right to determine whether an individual referred by the Ambassador is suitable for its purposes.

Payment Timetable
If this Agreement is terminated prior to the Campaign End Date (except where Lanistar has terminated under Section 9(i), (ii), (iii), (iv) or (v) of the Conditions below, where no further fees shall be payable or shares issued to the Ambassador), the Ambassador will be issued B Shares based on a pro rata proportion of the Fee (the " Pro Rata Fee") in respect of the Services satisfactorily provided to Lanistar to the date of termination (to be determined by Lanistar in its sole discretion). The number of the B Shares to be issued in such scenario shall be calculated by dividing the Pro Rata Fee by the Fee and multiplying the resultant fraction by the number of B Shares which would otherwise have been issued if the Agreement had not been terminated early, rounding down to the nearest whole number. These shares will be issued to the Ambassador, credited as fully paid, within 30 days of the later of the termination date and the Ambassador providing an invoice for the Services satisfactorily provided in the sum of the Pro Rata Fee.
