CLAIMING YOUR SHARES

If you introduce influencers to Lanistar, Lanistar may, in its discretion, award certain ‘C’ shares In Lanistar Limited to you. The issue of such shares is subject to you introducing influencers to Lanistar, who Lanistar (at its discretion) chooses to engage and only where those influencers continue to work with Lanistar for at least 6 months. We will keep you updated on whether we engage influencers you introduce to us. If we sign an agreement with them, we will let you know the start date.

If you are eligible, you will need to raise an invoice to Lanistar as detailed above and in the Referral Fee section of your agreement. The number of ‘C’ shares to be issued will be determined by Lanistar who will consider the number and nature of influencers introduced.

Payment of the above invoice is by the issue of ‘C’ shares in Lanistar Limited at their nominal value. Each share has a nominal value of £0.01, therefore 100 shares will be issued for each £1 of your invoice. For example:

  • for an invoice for a Fee of £100, 10,000 shares will be issued.

Based on a potential 5 Billion total valuation of Lanistar Limited each of the shares received would be worth £12.50 (E.g 10,000 shares x £12.50 = £125,000)

SHARE SPECIFICS

‘C’ Shares

• In total the ‘C’ shareholders will together own 2.5% of the total value of Lanistar.

• Accordingly, each of these ‘C’ shareholders will own a share of this 2.5%.

• Referrers will be issued with ‘C’ shares in Lanistar’s sole discretion.

SHARE SPECIFICS

‘C’ Shares

• In total the ‘C’ shareholders will together own 2.5% of the total value of Lanistar.

• Accordingly, each of these ‘C’ shareholders will own a share of this 2.5%.

• Referrers will be issued with ‘C’ shares in Lanistar’s sole discretion.

• Lanistar may serve notice to convert any ‘B’ or ‘C’ shares into worthless deferred shares in the event you are in material breach of your Referral Agreement.

• In the event of a sale of a majority of the A shares to a third party, the selling A shareholders will have the right to require all of the remaining shareholders to sell all of their shares to the buyer on substantially the same terms but with the aggregate amount payable to the C shareholders, being 2.5% of the overall consideration.

CHANGES IN YOUR POSITION

Should anything happen to you concerning:

• your financial stability and you are declared bankrupt, the Lanistar Board will determine the best course of action with respect to your shares. This may include transferring your shares to a Lanistar appointed third party at market value; or

• your health, your shares in Lanistar will pass to your beneficiaries in accordance with your will, or your next of kin (as applicable).

Please see the Lanistar Articles of Association for more information on the above, together with additional information on what you can do with your Lanistar shares.
