PAYMENT AND INVOICING TERMS
Referral Fee

f the Referrer refers [one or more] Potential Ambassadors to Lanistar during the term of this Agreement, and such Potential Ambassador goes on to enter a contract with Lanistar for the purposes of providing influencer marketing services ("Ambassador(s)"), Lanistar shall issue to the Referrer C shares of Â£0.01 in the capital of Lanistar at par as further described in the Term Sheet ("C Shares"). The number of C Shares to be issued to Referrer in respect of any such successful referral shall be determined by Lanistar in its sole discretion ("Referral Fee"). Any such issue of C Shares shall be subject to the conditions set out in the Payment Timetable detailed below and Referrer issuing Lanistar with an invoice for the nominal value of the shares, being the cash equivalent of the Referral Fee. Again, such invoice (the "Fee Invoice") should reflect the fact it is to be settled by the issuance to the Referrer of the proposed number of C Shares.

For the avoidance of doubt no Referral Fee shall be payable to the Referrer in respect of any individual already known to or in contact with Lanistar in relation to the actual or potential provision of influencer marketing services. Lanistar shall have the sole right to determine whether an individual referred by the Ambassador is suitable for its purposes.

Payment Timetable

The Referral Fee, where relevant, will be paid to the Referrer (in C Shares as above) 6 months after execution of the applicable individual's contract with Lanistar, provided that such contract has not been terminated for any reason and Lanistar is satisfied (acting reasonably) with such individual's performance, and provided the Referrer has delivered the Fee Invoice to Lanistar. For the purposes of the Referral Fee, Lanistar shall notify Referrer following execution of the applicable individual's contract with Lanistar (and such individual shall be noted on the Fee Invoice).

Expenses

Lanistar shall not be responsible for any costs or expenses incurred by the Referrer and/or any other third party in relation to Referrer's performance of this Agreement.
