CLAIMING YOUR SHARES

Invoices:

In order to be issued with the shares detailed below, you will need to raise an invoice to Lanistar as detailed in the Fee and/or Referral Bonus section of your agreement. Raising the invoice accurately is important to ensure we can issue you the shares you are entitled to.

When should you raise your invoice?

Your invoice for the Fee should be raised on signing your Ambassador Agreement with us Your invoice for the Referral Bonus should be raised once the relevant influencer has been engaged by Lanistar for 6 months (further details below).

How much should your invoice be for?

For the Fee:

  • [£100]

For the Referral Bonus, Lanistar will notify you how many shares you are to receive – please issue an invoice for £0.01 x the number of shares.

Fee Payment:

Payment of the above invoice for your Fee is by the issue of ‘B’ shares in Lanistar Limited at their nominal value. Each share has a nominal value of £0.01, therefore 100 shares will be issued for each £1 of your invoice. For example:

  • for an invoice for a Fee of £100 - 10,000 shares will be issued

Based on a potential 5 Billion total valuation of Lanistar Limited each of the shares received would be worth £12.50 (E.g 10,000 shares x £12.50 = £125,000)

Referral Bonus Payment:

If you introduce influencers to Lanistar, Lanistar may, in its discretion, award certain 'C' shares In Lanistar Limited to you. The issue of such shares is subject to you introducing any number of influencers to Lanistar, who Lanistar (at its discretion) chooses to engage and only where those influencers continue to work with Lanistar for at least 6 months. We will keep you updated on whether we engage influencers you introduce to us. If we sign an agreement with them, we will let you know the start date.

If you are eligible, you will need to raise an invoice to Lanistar as detailed above and in the Referral Bonus section of your agreement. The additional shares will be a different class of shares to those issued for the Fee; these will be ‘C’ shares. The number of 'C' shares to be issued will be determined by Lanistar who will consider the number and nature of influencers introduced.

The nominal value of the shares to be issued will be the same as that of the 'B' shares above.

SHARE SPECIFICS

'B' Shares
  • In total the 'B' shareholders will together own 2.5% of the total value of Lanistar.
  • Accordingly, each of these 'B' shareholders will own a share of this 2.5%
  • The total number of 'B' shares will be limited to 10,000,000 ‘B’ shares unless shareholders holding a majority of the 'B' Shares agree otherwise.
'C' Shares
  • In total the 'C' shareholders will together own 2.5% of the total value of Lanistar
  • Accordingly, each of these 'C' shareholders will own a share of this 2.5%.
  • Referring Ambassadors will be issued with 'C' shares in Lanistar’s sole discretion.
  • The total number of 'C' shares will be limited to 10,000,000 'C' shares unless shareholders holding a majority of the 'C' Shares agree otherwise.

To clarify, if you refer / introduce influencers who Lanistar chooses to work with, you may own a number of 'B' shares and a number of 'C' shares in Lanistar.

 

Further rights of 'B' Shares & 'C' Shares
  • Non-voting
  • Dividends paid (at such frequency as Lanistar reasonably determines) subject to the following apportionment: 2.5% split across all B shareholders of Lanistar (pro rata to their holding of B shares); and 2.5% split across all C shareholders of Lanistar (pro rata to their holding of C shares)
  • The shares being issued are for a long term investment but with potential to participate in a sale of the business
  • In the meantime, you will be entitled to sell or gift the shares to any willing recipient, other than a competitor of Lanistar, without restriction imposed by Lanistar
  • Lanistar may serve notice to convert any 'B' or 'C' shares into worthless deferred shares in the event you are in material breach of your Ambassador Agreement
  • In the event of a sale of a majority of the A shares to a third party, the selling A shareholders will have the right to require the remaining shareholders to sell all or some of their shares to the buyer on substantially the same terms but with the aggregate amount payable to the B shareholders and the C shareholders each being 2.5% of the overall sale proceeds (assuming a sale of 100% of Lanistar).

CHANGES IN YOUR POSITION

Should anything happen to you concerning:

  • your financial stability and you are declared bankrupt, the Lanistar Board will determine the best course of action with respect to your shares. This may include transferring your shares to a Lanistar appointed third party at market value; or
  • your health, your shares in Lanistar will pass to your beneficiaries in accordance with your will, or your next of kin (as applicable).

Please see the Lanistar Articles of Association for more information on the above, together with additional information on what you can do with your Lanistar shares.
